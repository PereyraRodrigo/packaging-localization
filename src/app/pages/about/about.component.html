Creative Production Company

A team of highly skilled producers, developers and marketing artists are at your service. Founded in 2009 WAYPOINT is a full service creative production company. Headquartered in Los Angeles with a second office in Buenos Aires, we are trusted by the biggest names in gaming. Our 5 departments consist of over 200 full time people working (mostly) remotely.

Over 13 years ago WAYPOINT started offering creative services with a smile to the game industry. A few years later we got into collectibles in a big way. The experiential division (E3 Fat Chocobo!) followed suit in 2017. In 2019 we brought an indie studio from Argentina into the fold to offer game and software development (Pinball!!). Last but not least our Ecommerce division is delighting fans all over the world since 2020. We are WAYPOINT.
